Sherlock Holmes

Background

Sherlock Holmes is a fictional detective created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. He is known for his brilliant analytical mind, his keen powers of observation, and his exceptional deductive reasoning.

Notable Cases

  • "A Study in Scarlet"
  • "The Hound of the Baskervilles"
  • "The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes"

Memorable Quotes

"When you have eliminated the impossible, whatever remains, however improbable, must be the truth."

"It is a capital mistake to theorize before one has data."
